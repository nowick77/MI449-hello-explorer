Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite and our closest celestial neighbor. Its gray, cratered surface tells the story of billions of years of asteroid and meteor impacts, frozen in time due to the absence of atmosphere and geological activity. The Moon's gravitational pull creates our ocean tides and has gradually slowed Earth's rotation over time.
